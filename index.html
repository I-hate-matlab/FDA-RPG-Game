Welcome to FDA Compliance Challenge!

How to Play:

  • Enter your name and select a product type (Device, Drug, Biologic).
  • Choose your submission type for bonus funding.
  • Roll the dice each turn to make a decision: Good, Neutral, or Bad.
  • Good choices earn XP, Funding, and Compliance points.
  • Neutral choices give smaller rewards; bad choices cause penalties.
  • Face Boss phases every 3 scenarios for extra rewards.
  • Game ends after all scenarios. Check the leaderboard!
